About Choufha Luxe

Welcome to Choufha Luxe – your legendary destination for coquette aesthetic fashion in Morocco. We bring you the most exquisite, carefully curated pieces that blend Moroccan charm with international coquette elegance.

🌸 Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully selected to make you feel special, feminine, and confident. From silk ribbon bags to heart-print tops, we have everything you need to complete your coquette wardrobe.

💕 Our mission is to empower women through fashion that celebrates softness, elegance, and individuality.

📍 Based in Casablanca, Morocco
✨ Founded with love in 2025
🌍 Shipping worldwide
